From the Secretary General

Dear Prospective Participants,

It is my great pleasure to invite you to join us for the sixth session of Amity International Model United Nations, AMIMUN'17, to be held at Amity University, Uttar Pradesh from Friday, January 20, to Sunday, January 22, 2017.

AMIMUN’17 seeks to bring together motivated and talented delegates from all corners of the world and provide a forum for meaningful debate on issues significant to the international community. I hope that AMIMUN’17 will be an opportunity for you to gain insight into the art of diplomacy, understand the value of collaboration and compromise, and build skills in public speaking, research, and debate. Above all, I hope that AMIMUN’17 will challenge and inspire you to take the lessons learned at conference beyond the committee room, continuing to grapple with the complex issues in the world today and work towards change.

I strongly encourage you to take full advantage of the opportunity brought forward by AMIMUN. I encourage you to be creative, test the limits of current international relations, while remaining realistic, learn as much as you can from this experience and bear those lessons in mind when entrusted with real decision making powers in your future careers.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to us with any questions or concerns. We look forward to welcoming you in January!

Sincerely,
Gurmehak Mann
Secretary-General
